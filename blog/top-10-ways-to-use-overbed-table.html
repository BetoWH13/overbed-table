Top 10 Ways to Use an Overbed Table Beyond the Bedroom

Discover how your overbed table can transform into a versatile piece of furniture that serves multiple purposes throughout your home. From a mobile workstation to a craft table, these creative uses will help you maximize your investment.

While overbed tables are traditionally designed for use in bedrooms, their adjustable height, mobility, and convenient surface make them incredibly versatile pieces of furniture. Here are ten innovative ways to use your overbed table throughout your home.

1. Mobile Home Office Workstation

Transform your overbed table into a flexible workspace:

  • Perfect for laptops and tablets
  • Adjustable height for sitting or standing work
  • Easy to move between rooms
  • Ideal for small apartments or shared spaces

Pro Tip: Look for models with side storage for office supplies and cables.

2. Portable Craft Station

Create a mobile crafting space:

  • Ideal for sewing, painting, and DIY projects
  • Height adjustment for different crafting needs
  • Easy to clean surface
  • Can be stored away when not in use

3. Mobile Entertainment Center

Use as a portable entertainment solution:

  • Perfect for tablets and small projectors
  • Ideal for gaming consoles
  • Can hold snacks and drinks
  • Adjustable viewing angles

4. Kitchen Helper Station

Extra kitchen workspace when you need it:

  • Additional prep surface
  • Mobile serving station
  • Extra storage for cooking tools
  • Perfect for small kitchens

5. Adjustable Reading Station

Create the perfect reading nook:

  • Adjustable height for comfortable reading
  • Space for books and reading materials
  • Can hold drinks and snacks
  • Works with any chair or sofa

6. Mobile Plant Stand

Perfect for indoor gardening:

  • Adjustable height for optimal sunlight
  • Easy to move plants for cleaning
  • Water-resistant surfaces on many models
  • Multiple plants on one surface

7. Presentation Stand

Ideal for small meetings or presentations:

  • Height adjustable for standing presentations
  • Mobile for different room setups
  • Space for presentation materials
  • Can hold a laptop and projector

8. Music Stand Alternative

Great for musicians:

  • Adjustable height for different instruments
  • Space for multiple music sheets
  • Room for small instruments
  • Mobile between practice spaces

9. Kids Activity Table

Perfect for children's activities:

  • Height adjustable for growing kids
  • Easy to clean surface
  • Mobile for different activities
  • Can be used for homework or art

10. Portable Outdoor Table

Take it outside (weather permitting):

  • Perfect for balcony or patio use
  • Mobile serving station for BBQs
  • Adjustable height for different chairs
  • Easy to clean and maintain

Choosing the Right Table for Multiple Uses

When selecting an overbed table for multiple purposes, consider:

  • Weight capacity for intended uses
  • Surface material durability
  • Mobility features (wheels, locks)
  • Storage options
  • Size and adjustability range
